Paddy Leaf Disease

Classification Project

Identifying Leaf Diseases

Our project focuses on classifying paddy leaf diseases, including Brown Spot, Leaf Blast, Leaf Blight, and Leaf Smut. By utilizing advanced image recognition techniques, we aim to enhance crop management and improve yields.

Brown Spot Disease

Understanding Symptoms

Impact on Crops

Brown Spot is characterized by dark brown spots on leaves, leading to reduced photosynthesis. Early detection is crucial for managing this disease effectively.

Leaf Blast

Recognizing the Threat

Preventing Yield Loss

Caused by *Magnaporthe oryzae*, Leaf Blast can severely impact rice yields. Understanding its symptoms can help farmers take timely action.

Leaf Blight

Identifying Leaf Issues

Mitigating Damage

Leaf Blight can lead to yellowing and dying leaf tissue. Early identification is essential for effective management strategies.

Leaf Smut

Understanding the Disease

Protecting Your Crops

Leaf Smut causes dark lesions on leaves, stressing the plant. Awareness and quick action can help minimize its impact.
