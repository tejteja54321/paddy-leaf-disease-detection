These are the Disease types

Brown Spot

Characterized by dark brown spots on leaves. Early detection is crucial to minimize yield loss.

Leaf Blast

Caused by *Magnaporthe oryzae*, leading to irregular lesions. Understanding its symptoms helps prevent yield loss.

Leaf Blight

Results in yellowing and dying leaf tissue. Early identification is essential for effective management.

Leaf Smut

Causes dark lesions on leaves. Awareness and prompt action can minimize its impact.

Healthy Leaf

These leaves show no signs of disease and are critical for ensuring plant health and productivity.
